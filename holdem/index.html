Texas Hold'em

No-Limit Texas Hold'em. Everyone gets two private hole cards. Five community cards come out face-up in the middle — the flop (3), the turn (1), and the river (1). Make your best five-card hand from any of your seven.

Betting goes around after the hole cards and after each community street. On your turn you can Check (if no one has bet), Call to match the bet, Bet/Raise, or Fold. Bet what you like up to your whole stack — this is no-limit. The two players left of the button post the small and big blind to start the pot.

If everyone but one folds, that player takes the pot. Otherwise it's a showdown — cups up, best hand wins. Ties split; all-ins make side pots.

Hand ranking, high to low: Straight Flush · Four of a Kind · Full House · Flush · Straight · Three of a Kind · Two Pair · Pair · High Card.

It's a cash table — start with 1,000 caps, blinds 10/20, and you top back up to 1,000 whenever you bust. Cass plays tight and Boone likes to bluff.
