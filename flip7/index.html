How to play Flip 7

The goal: bank points by flipping number cards — but every flip can wipe you out. First to 200 points wins.

Your turn: Hit to flip the next card onto your row, or Stay to bank what's showing and sit out the rest of the round.

Busting: flip a number you already have showing and you bust — zero points this round. The deck has one 1, two 2s, three 3s… up to twelve 12s (and a single 0), so big numbers pile up fast.

Flip 7: get seven different numbers showing and the round ends instantly — you score them all plus a 15-point bonus, and everyone else banks whatever they had showing.

The other cards:

  • +2 … +10 — bonus points added to your round score. They can't bust you and don't count toward your seven.
  • ×2 — doubles your number-card points (bonuses added after).
  • FREEZE — hand it to any player still in the round (yourself included). They bank their points immediately and are done.
  • FLIP 3 — hand it to any player still in. They must flip three cards in a row, come what may.
  • 2ND CHANCE — keep it; the next duplicate you flip is discarded instead of busting you. Only one per player — a spare goes to someone who needs it.

Scoring: when the round ends, everyone still standing banks their showing cards. Busted players bank nothing. Cass and Boone play the odds — the deck runs thin and they know it. Good luck out there, dweller.
