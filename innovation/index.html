Eras of the Wasteland

A wasteland-original civilization card game in the spirit of the great tableau-builders: rebuild the world from Era 1 (Dust) to Era 10 (New World), one scavenged tech at a time.

Your camp has five domains — WAR, CROPS, TECH, SALVAGE, CREED. Techs stack by domain; only the top tech is active. Every tech carries icons: ₵ Caps · ⚗ Chems · ⚙ Junk · ⌖ Ammo · ⚔ Guns · ☢ Rads.

Each turn, take two actions:

  • SCAVENGE — draw a tech of your highest active era.
  • BUILD — play a tech from your pack onto its domain stack.
  • ACTIVATE — fire a top tech's effect. Here's the trap: rivals with as many or more of its key icon join in on the good stuff (and word gets around — you scavenge a free tech if they do). Effects marked AT GUNPOINT instead hit every rival with fewer of that icon.
  • CLAIM LANDMARK — bank an era's landmark if your stash holds at least 5× its era in caps and your camp has a tech of that era or later. Five landmarks wins.

Stash is your buried wealth — stashed techs are worth their era in caps. SPREAD effects fan a domain stack left, right, or up, exposing extra icons on the covered techs — that's how camps get strong.

If anyone must scavenge past Era 10, the world is rebuilt: the game ends and the richest stash wins (landmarks break ties). Cass hoards, and Boone's demands come at gunpoint. Good luck out there, dweller.
