How to play Uno

The goal: be the first to empty your hand each round. The winner scores the cards everyone else is still holding (numbers at face value, action cards 20, wilds 50). First to 500 points wins the game.

Your turn: play a card that matches the top of the discard pile by color or by number/symbol — or play a wild. Can't (or won't)? Draw a card; if it's playable you may play it right away, otherwise your turn passes.

The troublemakers:

  • SKIP (⊘) — next player loses their turn.
  • REVERSE (⇄) — play direction flips (acts as a skip with two players).
  • +2 — next player draws two and is skipped.
  • WILD — play on anything, choose the new color.
  • WILD +4 — choose the color and the next player draws four and is skipped. (House rule: playable any time, no challenges.)

UNO: when a player is down to one card the table announces it — no penalties here, just the dread. Cass and Boone hoard their wilds and aim their +4s with intent. Good luck out there, dweller.
